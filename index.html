About Me

Dayarnab Baidya (Student Member, IEEE) received his B.Tech. degree in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Agartala in 2017. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal, India, where he is a member of the i-Lab Research Group.

His research interests span decision-making, control systems, optimization, electronic sensors and systems, flexible/printed and wearable electronics, wireless systems, Internet of Things (IoT), and machine learning. He has published 17 research articles in reputed journals and international conferences and has contributed to 2 book chapters.

In recognition of his academic excellence and research contributions, he was awarded the prestigious Prime Minister’s Research Fellowship (PMRF) in 2023. He actively serves as a reviewer for several renowned journals and conferences published by Springer, Elsevier, IEEE, and others.

Brief Bio

Mitradip Bhattacharjee (Senior Member, IEEE) received B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology (NIT) Agartala in 2013 and the Ph.D. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, India, in 2018. Subsequently, he joined the University of Glasgow, U.K., as a Postdoctoral Fellow. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department, Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal, India, where he is leading the i-Lab Research Group.

His research interests include electronic sensors and systems, biomedical engineering, bioelectronics, flexible/printed and wearable electronics, wireless systems, IoT, micro/nanoelectronics, and reconfigurable sensing antenna. He has authored more than 50 research articles in reputed journals/conferences and filed more than 15 national/international patents. He also has authored several book chapters/books to date. He is the recipient of several awards such as Marie-Curie Seal of Excellence Award by the European Commission in 2019, Nanochallange Award by PSG in 2018, Gandhian Young Technological Innovation Award at Rashtrapati Bhavan in 2016 among others. He served as the chair of IEEE sensors council young professionals in 2022.
